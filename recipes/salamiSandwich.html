Salami Sandwich

Description

This is a salami sandwhich with cheese and lettuce! It is eaten a lot by Luis.

Ingredients:

  • 3 slices of Salami
  • 2 pieces of white bread
  • 1 piece of lettuce
  • 2 slices of mozarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Grab your slices of salami and put them evenly on one slice of bread.
  2. Grab your slices of mozarella and place them on the other slice of bread.
  3. Put the slices of bread into the toaster oven and set to medium toast.
  4. Once done, put the lice of lettuce in between!
  5. Serve and enjoy!
