Arroz con Pollo

Description

This is a spanish dish that consists of yellow rice and chicken. You can add any salad that you want to it.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of yellow rice
  • 4 chicken legs
  • cut up veggies

Steps:

  1. Put rice in water to cook.
  2. Fry chicken legs in the air fryer.
  3. Cut up the vegetables of your choice.
  4. Add everything to where the rice was cooking.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
