Spaghetti

Description

This is an italian dish that consists of spaghetti and red tomato sauce. It is easy to cook a lot for many people to enjoy.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 box of noodles
  • Water to boil
  • Your favorite Red Sauce
  • Garlic

Steps:

  1. Put noodles in a bowl with water and set to medium temperature.
  2. Once noodles are soft to liking, drain water and seperate the noodles.
  3. In a different saucepan, add the Red Sauce along with little garlic and seasoning like salt and pepper.
  4. Add the sauce to the noodles and stir. Sauce can also be served on the side.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
